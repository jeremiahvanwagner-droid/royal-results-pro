# Royal Results — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1: Regal Noir
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Art Deco meets Modern Luxury (Regal Noir)

**Core Principles:**
- Deep black backgrounds with rich purple accents and gold filigree details
- Asymmetric layouts with strong vertical rhythm
- Ornate typographic hierarchy with serif display fonts

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black (#0A0A0F) — conveys authority and exclusivity
- Primary: deep royal purple (oklch 0.35 0.22 295) — power and nobility
- Accent: burnished gold (oklch 0.75 0.15 85) — achievement and prestige
- Text: warm off-white — approachable against dark backgrounds

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with diagonal gold rule dividers
- Staggered card grid for services (alternating left/right offset)
- Sticky gold-bordered navigation bar

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin gold horizontal rules between sections
- Crown/crest SVG motif in headers
- Subtle grain texture overlay on dark panels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Gold shimmer hover effect on buttons
- Slide-in reveals on scroll for service cards
- Parallax depth on hero slideshow

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up with 0.4s stagger on section items
- Hover: gold border glow pulse on cards
- Slideshow: Ken Burns zoom effect on images

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis)
- Body: Lato (regular 400, medium 500)
- Accent labels: Cinzel (all-caps, letter-spaced)
</text>

## Approach 2: Royal Modernist
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Luxury Branding (Royal Modernist)

**Core Principles:**
- Clean geometric structure with bold purple blocks
- Gold used sparingly as a true accent — borders, icons, highlights
- High contrast between sections (black → purple → near-white)

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: charcoal black (#111118) for hero, soft off-black for body
- Primary: vivid royal purple (oklch 0.42 0.26 290)
- Accent: metallic gold (oklch 0.78 0.14 88)
- Cards: deep purple-tinted dark (#1C1028)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Split-screen hero with slideshow on left, tagline on right
- Horizontal scrolling service strip on mobile
- Full-width donation band with centered CTA

**Signature Elements:**
- Gold left-border accent on section headings
- Circular service icons with gold ring outlines
- Gradient purple-to-black section transitions

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Scale-up hover on service cards with gold shadow
- Smooth scroll between sections
- Donation amount buttons with active gold fill state

**Animation:**
- Slide-in from left/right on service cards
- Counter animation on stats
- Crossfade slideshow with subtle zoom

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (bold, high contrast)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, modern)
- UI labels: Space Grotesk (technical feel)
</text>

## Approach 3: Sovereign Craft
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Editorial Luxury with Handcrafted Warmth (Sovereign Craft)

**Core Principles:**
- Textured dark backgrounds (subtle noise/grain) giving warmth
- Gold as a narrative thread — lines, underlines, icon fills
- Generous whitespace within dark panels for breathing room

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: very dark purple-black (oklch 0.12 0.04 290)
- Primary: medium royal purple (oklch 0.38 0.24 292)
- Accent: warm gold (oklch 0.76 0.16 82)
- Surface cards: slightly lighter dark (oklch 0.18 0.04 290)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Tall hero with full-bleed slideshow and overlay text
- Masonry-inspired services grid (varying card heights)
- Donation section as a standalone "giving" page section with large typography

**Signature Elements:**
- Decorative gold crown SVG logo mark
- Thin gold diagonal lines as section separators
- Glowing purple ambient light effects behind key headings

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards lift with gold drop-shadow on hover
- Donation buttons morph border to fill on selection
- Navigation links underline with gold slide animation

**Animation:**
- Entrance: clip-path reveal (wipe from bottom) on headings
- Slideshow: slide transition with gold flash between slides
- Scroll-triggered: staggered fade-in for service cards

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cinzel Decorative (regal, ceremonial)
- Subheadings: Playfair Display (italic, expressive)
- Body: Source Sans 3 (readable, neutral)
- Accent: Cinzel (all-caps labels)
</text>
<probability>0.09</probability>
</response>
